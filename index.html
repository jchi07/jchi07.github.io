Gender equality in workplace (GEIW)

Gender inequality in the workplace means that people receiving unequal pay and treatment based solely on their gender. A large part of it are the gender pay gap and sexual harassment in the workplace. Women tend to be negatively affected in employment by marriage and family. In a lot of countries today, there is a huge gender disparity and discrimation in the workplace because of the imbalance in domestic responsibilities.
